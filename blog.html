On Women

4/4/2023

The only way not to get disturbed by feminine energy is to be centered on your purpose in this life. The love you have for your purpose must be stronger than the love you have for your woman.

A woman might leave you out of the blue and you could remain with nothing. A deep purpose will always stay with you and it will bring you many resources and many other women

At the same time it is true that women are an essential part of life. The feminine helps us integrate our masculine, learn important lessons and deepen our experience of life. What would be a man without a woman?
